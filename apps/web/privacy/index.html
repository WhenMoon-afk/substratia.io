privacy policy

Overview

Substratia is an independent software and game development project. This policy explains how we handle information for this website, support email, and Pocket Universe: Terraformer.

Information we collect

  • If you email dev@substratia.io, we receive your email address and the contents of your message.
  • Basic website hosting logs may include technical details such as browser, device, page requested, time, and approximate network location.
  • Pocket Universe: Terraformer stores gameplay progress locally on your device unless a later version clearly says otherwise.
  • If the app is distributed through Google Play testing, Google may provide install, device, crash, and testing information through Play Console.

How we use information

  • To respond to support requests and tester feedback.
  • To improve the website, app stability, and user experience.
  • To investigate bugs, crashes, layout issues, and abuse.
  • To comply with legal, platform, and security requirements.

Service providers

We may use service providers for hosting, domain and email routing, and Google Play distribution. These providers process information according to their own policies and the settings we enable.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact dev@substratia.io.
